What is all this?

A resource for all of us

As an ex-university student I've been taught a lot of things, I've also forgotten a majority of them too. Normally this wouldn't be too bad, but occasionally I like to know these things for a project or to teach it to a friend. So I decided to create a resource where I could do both, and hopefully help anyone else along the way.

Types of content

Seeing as my fields of study were Computer and Data Science, the content here will mostly relate to that, if something that you want explored isn't on here yet, feel free to contact me on my Twitter: @le_epic_weasel, GitHub: @alt1f923 or Discord: @Дункан#5963.
